Your Grade is a B!

  How to Raise Your Grades Quickly in 5-Steps

  • Assess where you are in the semester and what you have left to do

  • Sit down and think about how you’ve been studying up until this point. Analyze what has worked and what hasn’t.

  • Ask your Professor for advice on how you can improve, and where you might have gone wrong.

  • Create a study schedule, get yourself organized and attend tutoring

  • Buckle down … and stay buckled down until the semester is over.
